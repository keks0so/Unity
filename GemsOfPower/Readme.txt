This game is an interactive labyrinth, in which with the help of a dialog system the player undergoes career guidance. In the maze there are 10 crystals, each of which stands for a different school subject, to exit the maze it is necessary to collect a minimum of 4 crystals, after exiting the maze the player must choose from all the collected crystals 4 that will help him to defeat the dragon, after which there is a final battle with the dragon, at the end of which the player is given full information about his strengths and general recommendations based on them
